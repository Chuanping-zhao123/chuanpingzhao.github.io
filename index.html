Research Experience
10/2021 — Present
Graduate Researcher — GPCR Signaling
Dept. of Neuroscience, The Scripps Research Institute, FL · Supervisor: Prof. Kirill Martemyanov
Identified 10 GPCRs significantly expressed in photoreceptors by RNA-seq. Developed an in vivo CRISPR-tagging approach in the neonatal retina to investigate GPCR localization. Probed their roles using physiological, pharmacological, and biochemical methods.
10/2021 — 04/2025
Graduate Researcher — SMART Editing
Dept. of Neuroscience, The Scripps Research Institute, FL · Supervisor: Prof. Kirill Martemyanov
Developed the SMART (Silently Mutate And Repair Template) method for HDR to overcome PAM constraints, extending the editing range by over 200 bp. Validated in vitro and in vivo; enabled editing of previously intractable genes in the mouse retina.
